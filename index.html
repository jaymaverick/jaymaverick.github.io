Case Study: Scaling Business From $8M to $20M AAR

Designed a marketing plan to take client business from overworked 7-figure company to scalable 8-figure company within 4 years.

Client runs a relationship coaching business with a low 7-ﬁgure AAR. 100% of client's sales currently come from one ﬂagship product, with Facebook Ads as being the one traﬃc source. Each prospect goes through a 45 minute sales call before being accepted as client. Huge overhead because of sizeable sales staff and low ROI on expensive sales process. I created a new scalable sales process so client would need less time to make more money, and enable the company to grow into an 8-figure company.

We used Wordpress and Thrive Themes to build the funnel. Because the new product required very little time investment and also drove sales to flagship product, daily ROI has increased from 10% to 35%, with room to improve. Client is on schedule to hit $20M AAR before 2024.
